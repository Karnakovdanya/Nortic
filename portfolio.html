Dragon’s Vow  IN DEVELOPMENT

Her courage, his fire, their legend. 

Story‑driven action adventure about a sworn bond between a human heroine and an ancient dragon. Players master duo‑combat, explore mythic ruins, and make choices that strengthen or test the vow while the world shifts around their alliance.

What we’re building now
  • Status: Active development with weekly demos, bug triage, and sprint polish goals.
  • Duo mechanics: Synced finishers, shared ultimate, stagger windows, risk‑reward positioning.
  • Combat readability: Clear parry/evade timing, tuned hit‑pause, adaptive camera, responsive VFX.
  • Visual identity: Custom scale shader, soft emissive highlights, layered fog, filmic grade.
  • Vertical slice: Hero scene targeting stable 60 FPS on PC/console with fallback LODs.
What’s next
  • Encounters: New enemy archetypes, behavior trees, counter patterns for the duo kit.
  • Narrative: Cutscenes, VO, and choices that strengthen or test the vow.
  • Performance: Material instancing, effect batching, streaming tweaks, GPU budgets.
  • Audio: Leitmotifs, layered impacts, adaptive score tied to vow intensity.
  • Playtests: Closed sessions, UX telemetry, iteration on clarity, flow, and difficulty.
